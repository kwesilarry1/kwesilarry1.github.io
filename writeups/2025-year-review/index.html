Introduction#

Do i even need an introduction? Maybe not.But even people who feel like they failed deserve to be honest about it. This is my 2025 recap in not more than 200 words.

Recap of the year#

2025 was not the year I wanted. I did not become cracked or even semi-cracked in application security or bug hunting, which was my main goal. For that reason, everything else feels small to me.

I actually did not achieve anything this year, and I was a shit person too, or I guess the only thing I achieved was to survive and see 2026. Though I got a few cybersecurity gigs, did some projects, passed some exams/certifications, and also got admission to study BSc. Cybersecurity at one of the best universities in Ghana, subjectively, it still feels like I missed the target.

I learned that progress without alignment feels like failure, and also surviving is not the same as winning, so I think Michael lied, but it is not nothing either.

A loser is defined by Andrew Tate as someone who does not yet have what they want or need, So yes I was a loser or a shit person this year, but I am alive, still learning, still hungry, and still here to try again. Cheers to more life and to a better write-up in 2026.

Thanks for reading this shit from a loser <3.
